How It Works

Set it up once. Let Hermes run the rest.

HermesWork runs continuously in the background, automating the tedious parts of your freelance ops so you never miss a follow-up or payment.

01
Connect your accounts
Link your Stripe account, Telegram bot, and wallet address. Takes under 5 minutes.
02
Add clients & invoices
Enter your clients and create invoices. Hermes sends them automatically and follows up on overdue ones.
03
Accept any payment
Clients pay via Stripe (cards) or x402 (USDC). Both rails are active, instant, and confirmed automatically.
04
Build your on-chain rep
Each confirmed payment mints an ERC-8004 credential — a tamper-proof badge of your professional track record.
Payment Rails

Fiat or crypto. Your client's choice.

Two payment rails, one seamless experience. Accept USD via Stripe or USDC via x402 — both are built in and ready from day one.
